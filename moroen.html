Moroen Series Book 1

An epic adventure into Moroen, the Land of Three Rulers. Follow Briar and her friends as they unravel a sinister mystery and risk it all to reveal the truth. This debut dystopian fantasy series will take you on a wild adventure!

Briar is a soldier, and a seasoned one at that. But when an unexpected attack is launched against her unit, it becomes a race against time, and fate, to uncover a dark and centuries old secret about their world.
